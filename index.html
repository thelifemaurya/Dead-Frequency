PROJECT NIGHTMARE

FACE YOUR DEEPEST FEARS

THE TRUTH BURNS

Arjun thought he was protecting Meera and Tara. In the silence of the abandoned mountain town, the static of a Geiger counter is the only pulse left. But memories are like radiation—invisible, silent, and deadly. Why do they keep repeating the same words? Why is the sky always red in Tara's drawings? Search for the truth, before the hallucinations consume what’s left of your mind.

SURVIVORS?

Arjun

A man fractured by guilt. He navigates the fog of reality, desperately trying to save a family that might already be lost to the shadows of the past.

Meera

The calm in the storm. Her caring nature masks a disturbing glitch—a repetitive echo of a woman shielding her husband from a truth too horrific to face.

Tara

The innocent observer. Her cryptic drawings of "The Black Hill" are the only map Arjun has left in this wasteland of broken memories.

GAMEPLAY

Experience psychological horror through minimal visuals and maximum atmosphere.

The Geiger Mechanic: Follow the "tik... tik... tik..." sound. The faster it pulses, the closer you are to the truth—or your demise. Use notes, photographs, and emergency radio broadcasts to piece together the 1986 disaster.

Echoes of Silence

Some memories don’t fade… they haunt.

Story

Arjun wakes up in a fog-covered mountain town with his wife Meera and daughter Tara. Everything seems normal… until reality begins to break. Strange ticking sounds echo in the air, and the town feels abandoned. As he explores deeper, he discovers a forgotten radiation disaster— and a truth his mind refuses to accept.

Characters

Arjun

A mentally disturbed father trapped between reality and hallucination.

Meera

A calm wife who repeats words, hiding a terrifying truth.

Tara

An innocent child whose drawings reveal hidden reality.

Gameplay

Explore abandoned locations, solve puzzles, and uncover hidden truths while experiencing psychological horror and hallucinations.

Features

Offline story-driven gameplay • Psychological horror • Minimal graphics • Android optimized

Atmosphere

“tik… tik… tik…” — The Geiger counter never stops. “Emergency evacuation… radiation levels rising…”
